Our Mission.

This project was started by 3 seniors at Winston Churchill High School for the 2019 Congressional App Challenge. We wanted to provide the most accurate football predictions so that avid NFL fans like ourselves can expect who comes out on top each game. This can be used for purposes like fantasy football leagues or betting against peers.
